Updated Datasets of ocean warming

Mar 15, 2019

We are working on updating the datasets from our PNAS paper fairly regularly (thanks to many comments andd suggestions from others).

Most recent updates includes:

  • extending back to 1870 (rather than 1871),

  • adding 2018

  • using Sea Surface Temperatures from NOAA Extended Reconstruction SSTsV4 (in addition to HadISST v2 and v1)

The datasets with errors (defined as one-SD, derived from perturbing the Green’s Functions and from the use of different SSTs) are here:

  • Ocean Heat Content 1870-2018

  • Thermosteric Sea Level 1870-2018
